Ingredients: • 1 cup sour cream (can substitute with Greek yogurt for a thicker texture) • 1 cucumber, grated and squeezed to remove excess water • 1 tablespoon lemon juice • 1 tablespoon olive oil • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced (or more if you like it garlicky) • 1 teaspoon dried or fresh dill (optional: substitute with mint) • ½ teaspoon salt • ¼ teaspoon black pepper Instructions: 1. Prepare the Cucumber – Grate the cucumber and squeeze out as much water as possible using a paper towel or clean kitchen towel. 2. Mix Ingredients – In a bowl, combine sour cream, grated cucumber, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper. Chill – Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors blend.
